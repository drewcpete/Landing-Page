About &rew!

Hi! My name is &rew Peterson and I'm learning how to program at Epicodus! For the past 9 year I've been working for Shannon & Wilson, a geotechnical engineering company, as a field and lab tecnhnician. While I primarily did construction materials quality assurance, my responsibilities also included construction observation, environmental sample collection, and lab management. I ran my own soils lab and was the lead construction materials tecnhnician on a $400mil remote rail extension outside of Wasilla, AK. In 2018 and 2019, I put a lab together and performed soils testing for approximately 7 months on the Hanford Nuclear Site outside of Richland, WA. Other projects included large scale gold mines, wind farms, remote village runways, water treatment facilites and large stormwater runoff facilites. I began working for the company in Fairbanks, AK and was also full-time employee at offices in Anchorage, AK and Denver, CO. I've also worked on projects for the company's offices in Sacramento, Portland, Seattle, and Pasco. I had been looking for a reason and a way to move back to Portland and change careers, and after my fathers health took a turn for the worse, I quit my job, packed up a U-Haul and returned to my homeland of Portland and enrolled at epicodus.
