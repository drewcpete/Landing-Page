About &rew!

Education and Early Work History

Hi! My name is &rew Peterson and I'm learning how to program at Epicodus! I attended Lakeridge High School in Lake Oswego, OR and graduated in 2006. After high school, I worked at a restaurant for about a year before the business dissolved. After that I got a sales job selling ergonomic furniture at a store called Relax the Back. We sold the nicest office furniture, beds, recliners and massage chairs on the market. I worked at Relax the Back from approximately 2007 to 2012. Tempur-Pedic awarded me with the highest honor they have, "Dream Team Salesman". The company sent out secret shoppers around the nation, and only two people recieved perfect scores; me and a gentlemen in El Paso, TX. While working at Relax the Back, I was enrolled at Portland Community College taking primarily botany and horticulture classes. After two years of part time school, I moved to Alaska and began working in construction related services.

Recent Work History

In 2011, I moved to Fairbanks, AK and began working for Shannon & Wilson, a geotechnical engineering company, as a field and lab tecnhnician. While I primarily did construction materials quality assurance, my responsibilities also included construction observation, environmental sample collection, and lab management. I ran my own soils lab and was the lead construction materials tecnhnician on a $400mil remote rail extension outside of Wasilla, AK. In 2018 and 2019, I put a lab together and performed soils testing for approximately 7 months on the Hanford Nuclear Site outside of Richland, WA. Other projects included large scale gold mines, wind farms, remote village runways, water treatment facilites and large stormwater runoff facilites. I began working for the company in Fairbanks, AK and was also full-time employee at offices in Anchorage, AK and Denver, CO. I worked on projects for the company's offices in Sacramento, Portland, Seattle, and Pasco. I had been looking for a reason and a way to move back to Portland and change careers, and after my fathers health took a turn for the worse, I quit my job, packed up a U-Haul and returned to my homeland of Portland and enrolled at epicodus.

Hobbies/Interests

For the last two years I have planned and hosted a rafting trip. This year, 9 of us floated down the Rogue River for 5 days, 4 nights. I had friends from Alaska, Colorado, Oregon, Washington, and New york all attend. Last year, 15 of us floated down the Deschutes River. Next year I'm hoping we can get a permit to do the Middle Fork of the Salmon River.
